About SDG 15

Protect, restore and promote sustainable use of terrestrial ecosystems, manage forests sustainably and halt biodiversity loss.

Global challenges

  • Deforestation, habitat fragmentation and unsustainable land use
  • Land degradation and desertification reducing productivity and resilience
  • Biodiversity loss from habitat destruction, pollution and invasive species
  • Illegal wildlife trade and overexploitation
