Benefits of protecting land

Healthy terrestrial ecosystems support biodiversity, climate regulation, livelihoods and disaster resilience.

Key benefits

  • Conserves biodiversity
  • Secures ecosystem services: pollination, soil fertility, water purification
  • Stores carbon and mitigates climate change
  • Supports food security and livelihoods
  • Reduces disaster risk and supports public health
