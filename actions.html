Actions You Can Take

Practical steps at home, in your community and in policy to protect land and biodiversity.

Individual

  • Choose sustainably produced food and forest products
  • Reduce waste, compost, favor plant-rich diets
  • Plant native species and restore local habitats
  • Join citizen science projects

Community & Policy

  • Support local restoration and protected areas
  • Advocate for sustainable land-use planning
  • Support fair tenure for indigenous communities
  • Volunteer or partner with conservation groups
